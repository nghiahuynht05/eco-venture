Tay Giang

Considered a "sleeping treasure", Tay Giang - a mountainous district of Quang Nam, Vietnam possesses brilliant beauty but is deeply nestled due to its unique terrain. Tay Giang is like a mysterious picture, where the majestic scenery of precious, primeval forests blends with the culture of keeping the forest alive. Now, Tay Giang is ready to wake up and welcome creatures passionate about exploration.
